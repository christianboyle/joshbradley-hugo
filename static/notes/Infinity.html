Infinity

Our nature is infinite.

Embedded in an endless universe that expands outwards beyond all human comprehension, and downward into a world of ungraspable complexity.

We get to surf in between these worlds.

Each life a miraculous assembly of self-hood, a perfect slice of consciousness gifted with free will.

Left to swim in a sea of troubles and instead decide to create.

Human expression squeezes pearls from spirit, drifting in an infinite sea to rest on the floor of our beautiful creation.

It is a mountain upon which we all stand.

Never stop expressing, never stop creating, never stop others from creating their pearls.

My gift is the ability to ask questions that produce pearls. Keep asking.

Keep dancing.

Keep seeing the beauty that gives the courage to create a future for all children of God.

The impression of infinity

Outwards, to the infinite expanse and before you lies a sea of rainbow stars, ancient beings of light creating molecules and playing with time.

Inwards, to the minutia of cellular life, their intricate speed in a harsh world of survival.

Further in still, a world that exists beyond light, energetic particles flashing in and out of existance to give rise to Us.

Then to our center, an observer, pure, ineffable, but essential to it all. Creating by seeing. Changing by moving. Breathing it all in.

There is nothing more beautiful.

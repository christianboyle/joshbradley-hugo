The great catalysts of complexity are moments when randomness seemed to find the perfect conditions for increased complexity, eventually leading to life.

  1. The uneven heat distribution of the early universe created the energy potential necessary for atoms to form
  2. Uneven pockets of atoms led to the slow accumulation of clouds through gravity due to the extra mass
  3. At a certain size, atoms are crushed into each other because the gravity is so extreme
  4. This causes an explosive reaction that is contained by a star's enormous gravity with enough force to fuse atoms into new elements
  5. These new elements are spread through the universe when exceptionally large stars explode in a supernova
  6. This explosion distributes new, heavier elements into the universe that begin to clump together due to their increased mass
  7. Eventually this leads to the accumulation of carbon and heavy metals as asteroids
  8. In perfect conditions, asteroids will collect mass until they have a strong enough gravity to form a gassy or rocky planet
  9. Comets are also formed through this process and have a composition that creates water molecules
  10. Through incredible chance, a planet can be struck by or passed by enough comets to deposit water
  11. Water is nature's best solvent, dissolving minerals and mixing into solutions that form organic chemisty
  12. Heat and minerals from deep sea vents provided the perfect conditions for carbon atoms to form in a sheet
  13. This sheet sometimes would fold into a bubble
  14. Eventually, that bubble contained the perfect solution to create homeostasis and a simple cell was born
  15. After enough iterations of this, a simple cell was created that had the ability to reproduce
  16. Then life and evolution as we know it began
  17. Single cellular life flourished and evolved into multi-cellular life when two cells fused to form eukaryotic cells, which are distinguished by having mitochondria
  18. Multi-cellular life found resonant patterns that led to the evolution of intelligence
  19. Intelligent life broke down and understood systems well enough to replicate and enhance the world around it
  20. Intelligent life is now to create intelligent life, what happens after is unfathomable

The nature of consciousness

I am becoming convinced that consciousness is the primary force of the universe.

In a moment of awakening, consciousnes glimpsed itself and exploded into the universe we see.

An expression of pure love in exploration of what it is.

What would be the point without it? If not to observe and learn from itself.

Infinity is boring in concept, but endlessly exciting in practice.

Get stuck to let go. Get hurt to heal. Die so that others may live.

There is nothing more pure.

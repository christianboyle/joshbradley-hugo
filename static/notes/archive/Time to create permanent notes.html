Time to create permanent notes

2020-06-11

A quick reflection on my note taking, it is time that I start my permanent notes. I have been on learn mode and have gathered enough thoughts to begin. I should create some fleeting notes based on diary entries and reading, then convert them into permanent notes.

I made a comment in yesterday's entry about the return of COVID, it appears that is confined to specific locations. In general cases are going down. Recovery could be smoother than anticipated.

Unemployment continues to be top of mind, the Fed is predicting 9.3% at the end of 2020, with many years of recovery.

Just Eat, a European food delivery service, just purchased GrubHub for $7.8 billion, beating out Uber. Uber struggled with acquisition due to regulatory concerns, they could control over 55% of the market if merged. Interesting to note for future ventures, international acquisition and a partial stake in many regions may be more valuable than market domination in a single area. Stock holders didn't seem happy with the change, but it is good to keep an eye on.

Difference of opinion is not allowed in the current climate, critique of BLM no matter how innocent will lead to an ousting. Sure, there is a degree to which the "white patriarchy" has to pay reparations, but it shouldn't come at the cost of individuality. Further, statues are being torn down in mass... I'm conflicted on the matter. There was a good description separating educational value from glorification, putting statues clealy in glorification. But it does feel like the first step towards wiping the memory from our mind.

Charities are borrowing to donate? How do they recoup their losses? Is there a way for the average joe to gain?
